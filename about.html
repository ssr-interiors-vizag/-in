Our Story & Philosophy

At SSR VIZAG INTERIORS, we don't just design rooms; we curate experiences that reflect your soul. Founded by BODDEDA NIRANJAN SAI, our journey began with a simple belief: A home should be a sanctuary of comfort and a reflection of personality. We bridge the gap between your dreams and reality, blending aesthetics with functionality.

Whether it is a cozy corner or a full home makeover, we listen to your story to create spaces that feel uniquely you. With 6 years of experience, we are a premier interior design firm specialising in luxurious, functional, and modern spaces. We take a holistic approach—meticulously planning from concept to execution to ensure precision, quality, and timeless style.

We work with you to transform residential and commercial properties, offering end-to-end solutions that elevate everyday living. We believe in transparency and passion, delivering results that stand the test of time. Let's create something beautiful together.
